Shark Tooth Showdown

6 Adventurers 2 Tracks 4 Keys 1 Buried Treasure

The Legend

The Story

Deep beneath these waters lives Captain Chomper — the most cunning, tooth-grinning, treasure-hoarding shark who ever prowled the shallows. She's enormous, she's dramatic, and she has buried her most prized collection of ancient teeth somewhere on this very beach. Captain Chomper never makes things easy. She left behind locks, riddles, hidden boxes, and fiendish puzzles to protect her stash. But legend says that six brave hunters who work together can outsmart even her... if they dare to try.

Overview

How It Works

For helpers: There are two simultaneous tracks. Track 1 sends the older kids on a field adventure. Track 2 keeps the younger kids busy with puzzles at the cabana. Both tracks finish at the same time and everyone digs for treasure together.
🚴
Track 1 — Field Quest
The two 11-year-olds follow GPS coordinates on a bike ride to the lookout tower and back. Independent adventure, big payoff. Yields Keys 1 and 2.
🧩
Track 2 — Puzzle Quest
The four younger girls solve escape room puzzles, open locks, and crack open mystery boxes at the cabana. Adult supervision recommended. Yields Keys 3 and 4.
🏖️
The Finale
All six kids converge at the cabana. Four keys, a treasure map, GPS coordinates, and a buried box full of shark teeth and prizes waiting in the sand.
⏱️
Timing
Each track takes roughly 45–75 minutes. Start both tracks simultaneously. If one finishes early, have them help set the mood for the finale dig.

For Helpers

Hints Guide

⚠️ Keep this page away from the kids! These hints are for adults to dispense gradually if adventurers get stuck. Start with the gentle hint — only go further if needed.
🚴 Track 1 — Field Quest Hints
🟢 Gentle
"Captain Chomper loves to hide things where they blend in. Look for something that seems like it belongs on the beach but doesn't quite."
🟡 Medium
"The geocache is designed to look like a natural beach object. Search carefully near the shoreline."
🔴 Direct
Walk them to the general area and say "it's within 10 feet of where you're standing."
🟢 Gentle
"The magnet from the shell guides a ball hidden inside the box. Hold it underneath and move it slowly — you'll feel the ball following it."
🟡 Medium
"The ball starts in the top right corner. You need to guide it through the maze walls all the way to the exit hole in the bottom right corner. Move the magnet slowly underneath — don't rush or the ball will get stuck."
🔴 Direct
Place the magnet under the top right corner to find the ball. Guide it left across the top, down the left side, right along the bottom channel, navigating each wall until it drops through the exit hole in the bottom right. Demonstrate slowly if needed.

🧩 Track 2 — Puzzle Quest Hints
🟢 Gentle
"Read the riddle out loud together. Each line is a clue — what picture does it paint in your mind?"
🟡 Medium
"The answer is something right here at the cabana. Look around — it's in plain sight."
🔴 Direct
Point them directly to where the egg puzzle box is hidden.
🟢 Setup notes
6 puzzles produce the full code: 7, 9, 8, 5, 7, 8. Kids must then remove the Hanger 98 lunch digits (9, 8) to get the final safe combo: 7 — 5 — 7 — 8. Set the cabana safe to 7578 before the adventure.
🟢 Gentle
"Use the arcade game picture as a decoder. Apply it to the message written on the bathtub."
🔴 Answer
Decoding the bathtub message using the arcade game picture gives the word "SEVEN" — so the digit is 7.
🟢 Gentle
"Look at the marble balls in the coloured hands — each colour corresponds to a number. The yellow picture tells you what to do with them."
🔴 Answer
Blue hand = 5, Green hand = 4. The yellow picture says to add Blue + Green: 5+4 = 9.
🟢 Gentle
"There are 2 pictures with matching frames. Each one spells a number. The dino picture tells you what to do with those two numbers."
🔴 Answer
One frame spells SIX, the other spells TWO. The dino picture says to add them: 6+2 = 8.
🟢 Gentle
"Look at the drawing on the duck — which digit is missing? Which one can't be shaped?"
🔴 Answer
The only digit that cannot be shaped in the duck drawing is 5.
🟢 Gentle
"Read only the second letter of each word in the letter. The hidden message will tell you exactly what to count."
🔴 Answer
Reading every second letter spells "THE ANSWER IS THE NUMBER OF EYES ON THE TEDDY-BEAR" — a teddy bear has 2 eyes, but count all eyes on the picture. The answer is 7.
🟢 Gentle
"Read the message on the green soldier picture backwards. Then look at the green squares on the Rubik's cube — green matches the soldier's colour."
🔴 Answer
The green squares on the Rubik's cube correspond to 5, 2, 1, and 0. Add them: 5+2+1+0 = 8. Full 6-digit code is 7,9,8,5,7,8. Remove 9 and 8 (Hanger 98) → safe combo is 7578.
🟢 Gentle
"This box has a secret. It's not about strength — it's about finding exactly the right angle."
🟡 Medium
"Try tilting it slowly in different directions. There's one position where something inside lines up and the box opens."
🔴 Direct
Demonstrate the correct angle/position needed to open the box.
🏖️ Finale Hints
🟢 Gentle

🟡 Medium

🔴 Direct
Walk them through the steps and lead them toward the second hammock area.

"X marks the spot — look for something that doesn't quite belong in the sand near the second hammock."
