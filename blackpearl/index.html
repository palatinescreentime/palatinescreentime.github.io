📍 Geocache Placement Guide

Best spots along the Castaway Cay bike trail: Look for a distinctive tree, a trail marker post, or a large rock cluster where a small waterproof container can be wedged out of plain sight but findable with the riddle hint.

Container: Use a small Lock & Lock container or a Pelican micro case. Line with a zip bag. Include: Key #3, a log sheet, a pencil, and a "Captain Jack" congratulations note.

For the clue: Give directional hints ("15 paces from the fork where the trail bends left, behind the roots of the palm that leans toward the sea").

🔐 The Cipher Box — Segment II Challenge

This 3D-printed cipher/puzzle box is brought from home and hidden at Cookie's BBQ. The crew must decode the combination to unlock it and retrieve Key #2. Here's a suggested cipher to use:

The Message (written on a note with the box):

"Each symbol below hides a number, 1 through 5. Solve the code — then turn the dials to match. Jack's key awaits the clever!"

The Code to crack (customize to your box's mechanism):

⚓ 💀 🌊 ⚔️
⚓= 3
💀= 1
🌊= 4
⚔️= 2
🦜= 5
🏴‍☠️= 6

💡 Customize the symbols and numbers to match your actual 3D-printed cipher box mechanism. Print the symbol key separately and leave it hidden nearby (under the table) as a second clue the crew must find first!

🎭 Running the Cipher Scene

1️⃣
Two-piece reveal — Hide the cipher box under the table AND the symbol key under a different table nearby. The crew must find BOTH before they can solve it. Adds 10 minutes of searching fun!
2️⃣
Assign roles — The 12-year-olds turn the dials. The 8–10 year olds read out the code. The 5–7 year olds shout the answers when they figure out a symbol. Everyone contributes!
3️⃣
Build the drama — Make everyone gather round and count down "3... 2... 1..." when the final dial is set. Ham it up when it clicks open!
4️⃣
Difficulty tip for younger crew — Pre-mark one or two of the dials with a small dot so younger kids can "guess" correctly and feel the victory. No one needs to know!

🖨️ 3D Print Notes

Search for "puzzle box combination lock STL" or "escape room cipher box 3D print" on Thingiverse or Printables. A 4-digit dial combination box works perfectly. Print in dark filament (black or dark brown) for maximum pirate aesthetic. Size: aim for something a child can hold in two hands.

⚓ The Captain's Logbook ⚓
🌅
Scout the island the morning of (before kids wake up) — Walk the route early to confirm all hiding spots are accessible. Hide the geocache, tape down clue #1, and bury the treasure chest. This takes ~30 mins and is 100% worth it.
🎭
Stay in character — you are also a pirate crew member — Refer to yourself as "First Mate" or "Quartermaster." Call the kids "the crew." Refer to the ship as "our vessel." The more you commit, the more magical it is for the little ones.
🌧️
Weatherproof everything — Double-bag every clue and the geocache contents. Seal with tape. Castaway Cay is breezy with occasional splashing — a soggy clue is a soggy adventure.
👧🏽
Manage the age gap actively — The 12-year-olds will want to race ahead. Give them a specific JOB: "You're the navigator — your job is to help the crew, not just yourself." This channels their energy into leadership rather than leaving younger kids behind.
🐣
The 3-year-old's role: Official Gold Coin Carrier — Give them a small pouch of chocolate gold coins and let them "hand out rewards" when the crew solves each clue. They'll feel essential and stay engaged without needing to solve riddles.
📸
Assign a "Ship's Photographer" — One of the 12-year-olds can be the official photographer. Photo challenges: everyone in pirate pose at each location, a photo when each key is found, and a treasure reveal group shot.
🔒
Test the cipher box at home first — Make sure younger kids can physically turn the dials. If it's too stiff, loosen it. Frustration kills momentum; it should be mentally challenging but physically easy.
🏁
Pre-wrap individual treasure bags inside the chest — Each child should have their own clearly labeled or color-coded bag of goodies inside the chest. No scrambling or disputes — everyone gets their own pirate haul simultaneously.
🎬
Record the chest opening — This will be THE moment of the day. Have someone ready to record video when the final key goes in the lock. You'll rewatch it every year.
