About Screen Time

Built by a Young Entrepreneur, Powered by a Big Idea

Screen Time was founded by our Chief Repair Officer, Nicholas — a young entrepreneur from Palatine who saw a real need in the community and decided to do something about it. Too many neighbors were living with torn and damaged window screens simply because fixing them was inconvenient.

The solution? Make it as effortless as possible. You pop the screens out, we handle everything else — pickup, expert re-screening, and delivery right back to your door.

But Screen Time is about more than just screens. It's a hands-on learning experience — building a real business, connecting with the community, exploring technology, and yes, earning some well-deserved spending money along the way.

Every job gets the same care and attention, backed by a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If something isn't right, we'll fix that too.
